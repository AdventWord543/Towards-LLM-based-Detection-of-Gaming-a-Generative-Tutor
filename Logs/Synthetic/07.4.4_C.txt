$$x + \sqrt{\frac{1}{x}} = \frac{1}{x}$$

$$\sqrt{\frac{1}{x}} = \frac{1}{x} - x$$

$$(\sqrt{\frac{1}{x}})^2 = \left(\frac{1}{x} - x\right)^2$$
